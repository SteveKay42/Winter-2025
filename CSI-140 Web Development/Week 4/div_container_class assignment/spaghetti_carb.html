Spaghetti Carbonara


Recipe

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.

Other Dishes to Serve With

Chicken carbonara is rich and creamy, so a light and fresh side dish would balance it nicely. Here are a few ideas:

  1. Garlic Parmesan Roasted Asparagus: It’s simple, flavorful, and complements the creamy pasta beautifully.
  2. Caesar Salad: Crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and tangy Caesar dressing add a refreshing crunch.
  3. Lemon Garlic Green Beans: A quick and easy side that’s zesty and bright.
  4. Caprese Salad: Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze add a touch of Italian flair.
  5. Sautéed Spinach: Lightly sautéed with garlic and a splash of lemon juice for a healthy, tasty side.
