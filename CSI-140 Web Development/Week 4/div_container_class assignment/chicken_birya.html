Chicken Biryani


Recipe

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt and spices. Let it sit for a while.
  2. Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice until it's about 70% done.
  3. Prepare Gravy: Saut√© onions, tomatoes, and marinated chicken. Cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Layering: In a large pot, layer cooked chicken and rice, adding fried onions, fresh herbs, and saffron milk in between..
  5. Dum Cooking: Cover the pot and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked and flavors meld together.

Other Dishes to Serve With

Five delicious dishes to serve with chicken biryani:

  1. Raita: A cooling yogurt-based side dish with cucumber, mint, or boondi.
  2. Papadum: Crispy, thin wafers made from lentil flour.
  3. Chutney: Mint, coriander, or tamarind chutney for an added flavor kick.
  4. Kebab: Seekh kebabs or shami kebabs for some extra protein.
  5. Gulab Jamun: A sweet dessert made from milk solids and syrup.
