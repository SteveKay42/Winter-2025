Top 10 Popular Recipes


1. Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy and rich Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients: Pasta, Eggs, Cheese, Pancetta, pepper

Instructions: Cook pasta, mix eggs and cheese, fry pancetta, and combine.


2. Chicken Biryani

A flavorful Indian rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice.

Ingredients: Chicken, Rice, Spices, Yogurt, Onions

Instructions: Marinate chicken, cook with spices, layer with rice, and steam.


3. Sushi Rolls

A Japanese delicacy made with seasoned rice, fresh seafood, and vegetables.

Ingredients: Rice, Seaweed, Fish, Avocado, Cucumber

Instructions: Prepare rice, roll with fillings, and slice.
